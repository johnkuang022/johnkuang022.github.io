About

"Horrendous Downplay" began as a pet name for Elvin Tanoy's (drums) experiments in music, but through creative collaboration with Star Bannister (vocals), Jack Shi (guitar), John Kuang (keys/synth), and Marialé Modesto (bass), it quickly blossomed into something greater. Their dynamic range of music spans from melow, dramatic folk ballads to unapologetically passionate rock anthems, all while remaining loyal to Tanoy's experimental and electronic roots. Their music and poetry speak of philosophy, depression, longing, and the beauty of life itself. Whether through an energetic rhythm section, overwhelmingly massive soundscapes, or powerful singing, their performances are both energetic and overwhelming, yet still intimate with the audience. In all aspects, their art captures the raw essence of what drives them. Each member hails from a different corner of the world, bringing their own uncharted perspective into their art. Their charismatic stage presence and creative synergy ensure that any stage, wherever it may be, will quiver with raw passion
